The Overwatch League is a competitive video game league featuring 20 city-based teams. The teams play 280 regular season matches in Blizzard Entertainment's game Overwatch, a team-based strategic shooter with unique heroes and abilities. A team is comprised of 6 players, all playing different heroes and roles.
Over three seasons, the Overwatch League has collected statistics on teams, players, and roles. Let's begin by looking at team win-loss records.
Tanks are an important role in a team. They can create space for damage players, change the tempo of the match, or protect the team with shields and barriers.
Support heroes keep the team alive and assist in making offensive plays. Often times, they are protecting their own lives while trying to keep their team alive.
Damage players, or DPS, have burst damage, require consistent aim, and can make key eliminations to give their team a player advantage on an objective.
